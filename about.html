About IExplore

Who we Are

IExplore is one of the famous Go Kart Arena located in Rayfield, Jos. We are not only known for just this but we are also recognised for other services we render which are : Sport Lounge, Food Court, Karaoke, Live Band, Grills and Bar, Clubhouse and Snooker Pool.

IExplore provides a cool and condusive environment for recreational activities. It also a place where you can have fun with friends and families.

We are located at Military vacated area, opposite Rayfield Golf Club, Jos which is one of the most peaceful place on the Plateau.
